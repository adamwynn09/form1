Security Systems

Use of the word security refers to the techniques used to ensure that data and files stored on a computer cannot be read or compromised by any individuals without the correct authorisation

Reasons for needing security

  • Protecting financial systems and currency
  • Avoid and stop identity theft
  • Protection of personal files
  • Ensure a sense of privacy when accessing internet

Cryptography

Cryptography is the practise of successfully encrypting and decrypting data.
This encryption turns the data into unintelligible text that is useless to malicious third parties, known as adversaries.
